ABOUT SCCIF

SCCIF is a Non-denominational but inter-denominational Christian foundation which seeks to develop the quality of life of people in most remote communities with key interest in helping the vulnerable, physically challenged and the poor, promoting education, youth development and empowerment, entrepreneurship, capacity building, strategic missions and the likes as we reach them with the gospel of Christ Jesus


REGISTRATION SCCIF is duly registered with Registrar of Companies under the Ghana Companies Code, 1963 (ACT 179) as a Christian foundation since 30th July, 2010 with the registration number, G-33,253. SCCIF has also registered with Department of Social Welfare with registration number, D.S.W./6216.

Mission and Vision

Mission Statement

To develop the quality of life of people in remote communities with key interest in the vulnerable and physically challenged by promoting education, youth development empowerment and entrepreneurship through capacity building and strategic mission with passion and commitment.

VISION STATEMENT

'FOR A CHRIST CENTERED DEVELOPED LIFE'
