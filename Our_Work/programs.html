Achievements

Needy; our free OCC gift box distribution to school children and our provision for the Soul Winning: Through missions about 100 souls have been won to Christ with conscious follow up on them. Educational Programmes: The impact of our educational programmes with the people of Agomor and Dawda over the past three years has brought great transformation in the lives of the youth and children in these areas. We recently supported a student in paying her admission fee into the Nursing School. Operation Care for the needy which include distributing of clothing, food, provisions, etc. over the past three years has put smiles in many faces and remarkable joy in many heart that will not be easily for gotten. Medical /Health Support Project; through this project SCCIF took total responsibility of the surgical operation of Raphael a boy who was suffering from “Osteomyelits” (Bone Infection) and lived over a year before passing away in October 2015, may his soul rest in perfect peace. We also organise medical screening for the entire community where we have our missions and donate drugs to support health services.

SCCIF Mission

It is a policy for SCCIF to embark on missions at one place for three (3) conservative years and SCCIF successfully completed a three years missions at Agomor in 2013 and started another life impacting missions at Ato Dauda from 2014 to 2016 and this year, we will be at Eastern Region. Over the past years of our mission trips, there has been several programmes and activities to impact lives at these communites. In summary, activities such as door-to-door evangelism, dawn broadcasting, open-air crusades, revivals, counselling, leadership/youth seminars, child evangelism, community forums/general assemblies, donation to the need, educational and medical support etc.

Prisons Mission

As part of our core mandate which is to embark on outreaches (Prisons visitation, Schools visitations and community outreaches), SCCIF embarked on its maiden Prisons visitation dubbed “Operation visit them, feed them and cloth them” under the theme “that I may know Him and the power of His resurrection at Ankaful Maximum Security Prisons on the 14th and 15th April 2017. Activities shall include; Small group evangelism and Alter call, Music and drama, Fun games, Feeding/Refreshments, Distribution of clothing, Donation (toiletries, provisions, detergents, food stuffs, Drugs etc.)
