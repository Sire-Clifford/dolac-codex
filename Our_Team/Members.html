Voluntary Members/Partners

Majority of the members of SCCIF are its voluntary members/ partners. Membership is opened to all and sundry who share in the vision and mission of SCCIF. Thus membership/ partners is made up of institution/organizations, community groups, SCCIF achievers club members, interest groups, as well as individuals and the like who share in the vision of SCCIF. Members/ partners are to registered with SCCIF. Voluntary members /partners shall belong to at least one of these commitments; Prayer Commitment, Financial Commitment, Self-Volunteer Services, Material Provision, Special Projects support, etc.
